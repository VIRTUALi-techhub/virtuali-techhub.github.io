About

What we are all about.

VIRTUALi is a startup registered under Government of Gujarat at SSIP I-HUB, KCG, Ahmedabad.

Our expertise in 3D hologram, IoT, AR/VR, Gaming, Startup support and Smart City Solutions.

We are working with our client in B2B and B2C. Our aim is to provide cost effective solutions by using latest technologies to make indigenous - Make in India product.

We work mostly with open source technologies and tools to provide more secure, cost effective and quality solutions to customer.

We plan to make this startup to become technology hub. VIRTUALi is following Hon. Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi 's vision regarding "Startup India" and "Digital India".
